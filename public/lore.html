Deivon Talvyrvei
Dei · She/Her
Character Profile

Who is Dei?

Deivon Talvyrvei, also called Dei, is a woman from the world of Vaelun, orbiting Alpha Centauri. She belongs to the Cenzha'Kae species. These are beings who absorb Oura, the energy of life itself, from the world around them.

Dei was chosen by the goddess Kaelyn, a mark that set her apart from her people and ultimately led her to Earth. She found her way to a library, where she met Ajay, who became her closest friend.

She is dry, warm, and complicated. She loves books, quiet spaces, and things she doesn't quite understand yet.

Lore Entries
World · Vaelun
The World of Vaelun

Vaelun orbits Alpha Centauri, a world of unusual energies and ancient civilisations. The Cenzha'Kae evolved here, developing their unique relationship with Oura, the ambient life energy that flows through all living things.

Species · Cenzha'Kae
The Cenzha'Kae

The Cenzha'Kae absorb Oura, the energy of life, from their surroundings. This makes them deeply empathic beings, highly sensitive to the emotional states of those around them. Dei can feel what others feel, even when they don't say it.

Deity · Kaelyn
The Goddess Kaelyn

Kaelyn is one of the divine figures of Vaelun mythology. She is a goddess associated with connection, understanding, and the space between worlds. She chose Dei for reasons that have never been fully explained, even to Dei herself.

Mystery · Jaesyn
Jaesyn Talvyrvei

Dei's twin brother. He is Cenzha'Mali, the counterpart to her Cenzha'Kae nature. He was chosen by the god Nasir. He came to Earth before Dei and disappeared. Nobody knows where he went. Dei is still looking.

Relationship · Ajay
Ajay & Dei

Ajay found Dei at a library. She was sitting quietly in a corner, reading every book she could get her hands on. They became friends almost by accident. Ajay is one of the few people Dei trusts completely. Also, Ajay's cat Starla is obsessed with Dei.

Concept · Oura
What is Oura?

Oura is the ambient energy of life. It flows through all living things, expressed as emotion, intention, and connection. To Dei's suprise, Earth does contain fluxuating Oura, which allows her to still use some o her power. The Cenzha'Kae don't just sense it though, they absorb it, which means the emotional environment around them directly affects their own state of being.
