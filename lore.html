Dei · She/Her
Character Profile

Who is Dei?

Deivon Talvyrvei, also known as Dei, is an alien from the world called Vaelun. She and her twin brother, Jaesyn, crash landed on Earth a long ime ago! She is a long time project of mine as well as my persona online!

Dei was chosen by the goddess Kaelyn, a mark that set her apart from her people and ultimately led her to Earth. She found her way to a library, where she met Ajay, who became her closest friend.

She is dry, warm, and complicated. She loves books, quiet spaces, and things she doesn't quite understand yet.

Lore Entries
World • Vaelun
The World of Vaelun

Vaelun orbits Alpha Centauri, a world of unusual energies and ancient civilisations. The Cenzha'Kae evolved here, developing their unique relationship with Oura, the ambient life energy that flows through all living things.

Species • Cenzha'Kae
The Cenzha'Kae

The Cenzha'Kae absorb Oura, the energy of life, from their surroundings. Surpisingly, Dei found that there are fluxuating amounts of this energy on Earth, allowing her and her brother to use their powers. This makes them deeply empathic beings, highly sensitive to the emotional states of those around them. Dei can feel what others feel, even when they don't say it.

Deity • Kaelyn
The Goddess Kaelyn

Kaelyn is one of the divine figures of Vaelun mythology, a goddess associated with connection, understanding, and the space between worlds. She chose Dei for reasons that have never been fully explained, even to Dei herself.

Sibling • Jaesyn
Jaesyn Talvyrvei

Dei's twin brother. Cenzha'Mali, the counterpart to her Cenzha'Kae nature. He was chosen by the god Nasir. He came to Earth before Dei and disappeared. Nobody knows where he went. Dei is still looking.

Relationship • Ajay
Ajay & Dei

Ajay found Dei at a library, sitting quietly in a corner, reading every book she could get her hands on. They became friends almost by accident. Ajay is one of the few people Dei trusts completely. Also, Ajay's cat Starla is obsessed with Dei.

Concept • Oura
What is Oura?

Oura is the ambient energy of life. It flows through all living things, expressed as emotion, intention, and connection. The Cenzha'Kae don't just sense it, they absorb it. This means the emotional environment around them directly affects their own state of being.
