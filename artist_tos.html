By commissioning me, you agree to the terms outlined on this page in full. Please read this carefully before submitting an inquiry. It protects both of us and helps ensure a smooth working relationship.

1. General

Commissions are for personal use only. Artwork I create can be used as profile pictures/banners, but may not be used for profit. At this time, I will not do commercial licenses.

  • I own all rights to the work. Putting my work into any AI engine is prohibited. I am allowed to post on my social media unless requested otherwise. I am also allowed to include the artwork in my portfolio. You are however allowed to use my work of your OC as reference to commission other artists!
  • Listed prices are the base price. Please understand that the price may increase depending on additional details and the complexity of the commission.
  • I have every right to deny a commission if I feel that my skill level may not meet your request or if the request falls under a subject I will not do.
  • Please be patient with me for your commission to be finished. I do not accept rush orders due to my chaotic schedule. Please give me at most three (3) months max to complete the commission in case something happens. I value the quality and detail in my pieces, so I put many hours into my drawings. All I ask is for patience. You are welcome to ask for updates if I have not given you one in a while. I will try to give you an update at every stage of the piece, including rough sketch, line art, flat colors, and final check phases.
  • Please provide visual references. Many are encouraged and be detailed in what you want. Pose references are highly preferred. Providing a ton of details and references allows me to better fulfill your request and avoid unnecessary mistakes in the process. I would also like any palettes you would like me to work with, so I do not have to guess colors.

All commissions are completed by Ajay Hunter, an independent freelance digital artist. By submitting a commission inquiry and proceeding with payment, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms.

4. The Commission Process

  • Once your inquiry is received, I will review it and get back to you within 1-2 days.
  • After we agree on scope and price, payment is collected and work begins.
  • Upon starting, I will send progress images as I go. It will start with the rough sketch, in which, if you need me to tweak anything majorly, please let me know.
  • Once you let me know it is okay, I will start the coloring process. Once I start, it is quite difficult for me to tweak much. I will send your progress along the way.
  • Once I am finished, I will send one final check for your review. At this point, I will take five (5) minor revisions, and I have the right to deny a revision I feel to be major.
  • Once the finalized artwork is accepted, I will send you a clean .png and one with a watermark for social media purposes, as well as any other media that goes with the commission. There will be no physical product.
