About Ajay

AH

Ajay Hunter

Digital Art & Design
with Soul

Hi, I'm Ajay — a freelance digital artist based online, specialising in character art, illustration, and graphic design. I've been drawing for as long as I can remember, and turning that love into something I can share with others has been one of the best decisions I've ever made.

Every commission I take on is treated like a little world of its own. Whether it's your original character, a scene from your story, or a graphic for your brand, I put real time and heart into understanding what you want — and then making it better than you imagined.

Off-screen, you'll find me at the library (it's my favourite place), wearing something from the 1950s, reading fantasy novels, and being supervised by Starla, my black and white cat who has very strong opinions about my workspace.
